# Learning Outcomes

By the end of the curriculum, a prepared student can:

1. explain every concept explicitly listed in the NOAI syllabus;
2. solve Round 1-style multiple-choice, short-answer, calculation, and code-completion questions;
3. independently build a complete sklearn workflow;
4. independently build a small PyTorch training and validation loop;
5. create reliable baselines for tabular, image, text, and audio tasks;
6. analyse errors and choose the next experiment rationally;
7. use documentation and an LLM without submitting work they cannot explain;
8. compete under realistic time and compute constraints.
