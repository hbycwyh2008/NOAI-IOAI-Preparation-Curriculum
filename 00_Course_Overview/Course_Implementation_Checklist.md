# Course Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Teacher has access to the official current syllabus.
- [ ] Student and teacher repositories are separated.
- [ ] Students have GitHub, Python/Jupyter, and Bohrium access.
- [ ] Entry diagnostic is completed.
- [ ] Every lesson has one required resource.
- [ ] Round 1 paper practice begins before all theory is finished.
- [ ] Students complete at least six end-to-end notebooks.
- [ ] At least two timed Round 1 mocks are administered.
- [ ] At least two timed Round 2 mocks are administered.
- [ ] Final readiness conference is completed for each student.
