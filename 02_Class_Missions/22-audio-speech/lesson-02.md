# Mission 22.2 — Audio Classification, ASR, and TTS

## Required Resource
Hugging Face Audio Course: Audio Classification and ASR.

## Learning Targets
- build a Mel-spectrogram classification baseline;
- distinguish audio classification, ASR, and TTS;
- choose appropriate labels and metrics;
- diagnose duration and sample-rate problems.

## Independent Task
Build a compact audio classifier and produce an error table by class and duration.
