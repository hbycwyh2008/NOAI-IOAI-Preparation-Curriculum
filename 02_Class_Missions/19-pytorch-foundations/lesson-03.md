**Duration:** 90 minutes

## Required Resource
PyTorch Learn the Basics: Optimization and Save/Load.

## Learning Targets
- implement train and validation loops;
- use `model.train()` and `model.eval()` correctly;
- save and restore the best checkpoint;
- explain mixed precision and when it helps.

## Independent Task
Rebuild the complete loop without copying and verify a fresh-runtime run.
