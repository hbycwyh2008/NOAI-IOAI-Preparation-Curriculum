# Mission 04.2 — Bias, Privacy, Safety, and Responsibility

## Required Resource
AI for Everyone: AI and Society.

## Learning Targets
- identify data and algorithmic bias;
- distinguish privacy, security, fairness, and accountability;
- propose realistic mitigations.

## Guided Practice
Analyse two AI-system scenarios using cause, impact, evidence, and mitigation.

## Independent Task
Write a structured NOAI-style response to a new ethics case.
