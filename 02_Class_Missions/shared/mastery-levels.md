# Mastery Levels

| Level | Descriptor | Observable Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Not submitted | no usable evidence |
| 1 | Recognize | identifies terms or patterns |
| 2 | Explain | explains accurately in own words |
| 3 | Calculate / Trace | completes calculations or traces processes |
| 4 | Rebuild / Debug | reconstructs and repairs independently |
| 5 | Modify / Apply | transfers to a new setting |
| 6 | Compete | succeeds under time and compute constraints |
