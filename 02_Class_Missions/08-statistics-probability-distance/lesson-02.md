# Mission 08.2 — Normal Distribution, Distance, and Standardisation

## Required Resource
StatQuest: Normal Distribution, Standardization, and Distance.

## Learning Targets
- interpret the 68–95–99.7 rule;
- calculate simple Euclidean distances;
- explain why scale affects distance-based methods;
- standardise a small dataset by hand and in Python.

## Independent Task
Compare nearest neighbours before and after standardisation and explain the change.
