**Duration:** 90 minutes

## Required Resource
Andrew Ng MLS: Neural Networks and Forward Propagation.

## Learning Targets
- distinguish input, hidden, and output layers;
- calculate a small forward pass;
- explain why nonlinear activations matter;
- match sigmoid, ReLU, and linear outputs to tasks.

## Independent Task
Calculate and diagram a two-layer network, including tensor shapes.
