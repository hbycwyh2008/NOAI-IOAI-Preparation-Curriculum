**Duration:** 90 minutes

## Required Resource
Deep Learning Specialization: Sequence Models.

## Learning Targets
- explain recurrence and hidden state;
- describe vanishing gradients;
- explain the purpose of LSTM gates;
- trace sequence and batch dimensions.

## Independent Task
Implement or complete a small sequence classifier and explain every tensor shape.
