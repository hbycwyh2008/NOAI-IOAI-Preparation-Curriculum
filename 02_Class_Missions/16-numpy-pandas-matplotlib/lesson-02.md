**Duration:** 90 minutes

## Required Resource
IBM Data Analysis with Python: Data Wrangling and EDA.

## Learning Targets
- inspect schema, dtypes, duplicates, and missing values;
- filter, group, merge, and aggregate;
- choose an appropriate plot;
- explain what a plot does and does not show.

## Independent Task
Audit and visualise an unfamiliar CSV from a fresh notebook.
